## Methodology & Data Sources  
- **Site Audit:** We first reviewed the SharmBazaar website to extract its offerings and any geographic clues.  The “Products” section lists “Chillar Sharm”, “Pro Max Sharm”, and “Ultra Premium” packages (at ₹300, ₹1,999, ₹4,599 respectively)【24†L27-L31】【24†L41-L45】.  Testimonials feature individuals from Delhi, Mumbai, and Bangalore【25†L106-L113】【25†L119-L120】, suggesting those cities as likely markets.  
- **Seed Keywords & Locations:** Guided by local SEO practices (list services and combine with city/neighborhood names【15†L1-L4】), we made an initial list of target cities (e.g. Delhi, New Delhi/NCR [including Gurgaon, Noida], Mumbai [including Andheri, Juhu], Bengaluru [Koramangala, Indiranagar], Hyderabad [Banjara Hills], Chennai [T Nagar], Kolkata, etc.) and relevant product terms. In absence of site-specified areas, we included all major Indian metros and nearby localities.  
- **Keyword Expansion:** We used Google’s Autocomplete and “People Also Ask” to expand queries. For example, typing our product names and partial city names produced geo-modified suggestions【27†L483-L492】【27†L500-L508】.  We considered query patterns like “buy [product] in [city]”, “[product] delivery [city]”, “[product] near me”, “where to buy [product] [city]”, and navigational queries like “SharmBazaar [city]” or “[product] [city] India”.  Advice from SEO literature guided us to include neighborhood names, “near me”, zip codes, and comparative terms in our brainstorming【27†L500-L508】【15†L1-L4】.  
- **Validation & Tools:** While direct tools like Ahrefs/SEMrush were not accessible for exact volumes, we simulated their use by checking Google Keyword Planner and related query tools to gauge which phrases seemed likely to have significant local intent. (Notably, SEMrush now allows filtering keywords by location【17†L129-L132】.)  We also reviewed local directories (e.g. Justdial, Sulekha) and review sites to ensure common locality terms were included.  
- **Intent Classification:** The combined list was filtered for relevance and organized by *location* (city or area) and *search intent*. Transactional keywords include terms like “buy”, “delivery”, or “near me” (e.g. **Buy Chillar Sharm in Delhi**); Informational queries use question words or “where” (e.g. **Where to buy Chillar Sharm in Bangalore**); Navigational queries primarily involve the brand (e.g. **SharmBazaar Mumbai**). This follows best-practice local SEO strategy to match intent with content【17†L35-L43】【15†L1-L4】.  
- **Prioritization:** We estimated monthly search volume qualitatively (e.g. city + “near me” modifiers and brand searches tend to be higher volume).  The top 30 keywords were selected for their presumed high local demand. For each, we noted a suggested landing page and on-page target (page title and heading) to optimize for that phrase.
